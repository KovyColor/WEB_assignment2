About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo (born 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. He has won numerous Ballon d'Or awards, Champions League titles, and holds records for goals scored in club and international football.

Important Events

  1. 1985 – Born in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal
  2. 2003 – Joined Manchester United
  3. 2009 – Signed with Real Madrid (world record transfer fee)
  4. 2016 – Won UEFA Euro with Portugal
  5. 2018 – Signed with Juventus
  6. 2021 – Returned to Manchester United

Key Achievements

  • 5× Ballon d'Or winner
  • 5× UEFA Champions League winner
  • Most goals in Champions League history
  • European Championship winner (2016)
